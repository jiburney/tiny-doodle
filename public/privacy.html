Privacy Policy

Tiny Doodle is designed with your child's privacy in mind. We collect NO personal information. All drawings are saved on your device only. We use anonymous analytics to improve the app (COPPA compliant). No accounts, no ads.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tiny Doodle! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our drawing application. Tiny Doodle is committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children under the age of 13.

Tiny Doodle is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

2. Information We Collect (Anonymous Analytics Only)

Tiny Doodle uses anonymous analytics to understand how the app is used and improve the experience. This analytics is:

  • Fully COPPA Compliant: No personal information is collected
  • Anonymous: We cannot identify individual users
  • Aggregated: Data is combined with all other users

What Analytics Data We Collect:

  • App Usage: Page views, session duration, feature usage (e.g., "save drawing button clicked")
  • Technical Data: Browser type, screen size, device type (phone/tablet/desktop), operating system
  • Interaction Events: Actions like "opened gallery", "used undo", "changed color" (no content of drawings is collected)

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal Information: No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifying information
  • Location Data: No GPS coordinates, city, state, or country information
  • IP Addresses: Not stored or tracked
  • Drawing Content: We never see what you draw
  • Photos or Camera Access: We do not access your camera, photo library, or any media files
  • Contacts: We do not access your contact list

Analytics Provider:

We use Countly (self-hosted) for analytics, configured with strict privacy settings:

  • No personal data collection enabled
  • No cross-site tracking
  • Data is anonymized and aggregated
  • You cannot opt-out of analytics, but since no personal data is collected, this remains COPPA compliant

3. How Drawings Are Stored

All drawings created in Tiny Doodle are stored locally on your device only:

  • Web Version (PWA): Drawings are saved in your browser's local storage. They never leave your device unless you explicitly share them using the Share button.
  • iOS Native App (Planned): Drawings will be saved in the app's local storage on your iPhone or iPad. No cloud sync, no external servers.

Important: If you clear your browser's cache or delete the app, all saved drawings will be permanently deleted. We recommend using the Share button to save important drawings to your photo library.

4. Sharing Features

Tiny Doodle includes a Share button that allows you to:

  • Save drawings to your device's photo library
  • Share drawings via messaging apps, email, or social media

How sharing works: When you tap the Share button, your drawing is converted to an image file and shared using your device's native sharing features. We do not upload, store, or see what you share. The sharing is handled entirely by your operating system (iOS, Android, or browser).

5. Third-Party Services

Analytics: We use Countly (self-hosted) for anonymous, COPPA-compliant usage analytics as described in Section 2.

Tiny Doodle does NOT use any third-party services for:

  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integration
  • Cloud storage
  • Payment processing (the app is free)
  • Behavioral tracking or profiling

Web Hosting: The web version is hosted on Netlify, a secure web hosting service. Netlify may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, timestamps) for security and performance purposes, but this data is not accessible to us and is not used for tracking individual users.

6. Internet Connection

Tiny Doodle does NOT require an internet connection to function:

  • After initial installation: The app works completely offline
  • No data transmission: No information is sent to external servers
  • No updates or notifications: The app does not check for updates or send notifications while in use

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Tiny Doodle is designed for children of all ages, including children under 13. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • No personal information collection: We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13
  • No accounts required: Children can use the app without creating an account or providing any information
  • No behavioral tracking: We do not track, monitor, or profile children's behavior
  • Parental control: Parents can delete all app data at any time by clearing browser storage or deleting the app
  • No targeted advertising: We do not display any advertisements

Parent Rights: Parents have the right to review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's personal information. Since we collect NO personal information, there is nothing to review or delete from our end. All data is stored locally on your device.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your drawings are as secure as your device's security settings
  • No data is transmitted over the internet (except when you explicitly share)
  • No risk of data breaches from our servers (because we don't have servers storing your data)

Recommendation: Use device passcodes, Face ID, or Touch ID to protect your device and keep your drawings private.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify users through the app or website
  • Never reduce privacy protections without explicit user consent

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

10. Your Rights

Because we don't collect any personal data, you automatically have:

  • Right to Access: All your data (drawings) is already accessible to you on your device
  • Right to Deletion: You can delete all data by clearing browser storage or uninstalling the app
  • Right to Opt-Out: There's nothing to opt out of since we don't track or collect data
  • Right to Portability: You can export drawings using the Share button

11. International Users

Tiny Doodle can be used anywhere in the world. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • No data transfers across international borders
  • Compliant with GDPR (Europe), CCPA (California), and other privacy regulations
  • No regional restrictions or geo-blocking

12. Future Features

If we add new features that require data collection (such as cloud sync or social features), we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy before implementing those features
  • Make those features opt-in only
  • Obtain explicit parental consent for children under 13 (COPPA requirement)
  • Provide clear, age-appropriate explanations of what data will be collected
