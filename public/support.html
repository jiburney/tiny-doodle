‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Tiny Doodle really free?
Yes! Tiny Doodle is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no hidden costs. We believe creative tools should be accessible to everyone.
Q: Is my child's data safe?
Absolutely! We collect NO personal information. All drawings are saved locally on your device. We don't track usage, collect emails, or share any data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Q: Does Tiny Doodle work offline?
Yes! After the initial load, Tiny Doodle works completely offline. Perfect for long car rides, flights, or anywhere without internet.
Q: How do I install Tiny Doodle on my iPhone/iPad?
1. Open Tiny Doodle in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"
The app icon will appear on your home screen like a regular app!
Q: Can I share my drawings?
Yes! Tap the Share button (‚Üó) in the bottom tray to save drawings to your photo library or share via Messages, Email, or social media.
Q: Where are my saved drawings?
Tap the Gallery button (üìÅ) in the top-right corner to view all your saved drawings. Drawings are stored in your browser's local storage.
Q: Will there be an iOS app?
Yes! We're working on a native iOS app for iPhone and iPad. It will be available on the App Store in 2026. The web version will continue to be maintained.

üîß Troubleshooting

App won't load or is stuck

Step 1: Refresh the page (swipe down or press F5)
Step 2: Clear your browser cache (Settings ‚Üí Safari ‚Üí Clear History and Website Data)
Step 3: Try a different browser (Safari is recommended for iOS)

Drawings won't save

Check storage: Make sure your device has available storage space
Browser settings: Ensure cookies/local storage is enabled in your browser settings
Private browsing: Exit private/incognito mode (data doesn't persist in these modes)

Touch/drawing not working

Browser compatibility: Use Safari on iOS, Chrome on Android, or any modern browser on desktop
Close other apps: Some apps may interfere with touch events
Restart device: Sometimes a simple restart fixes touch issues

Lost drawings after clearing cache

Unfortunately: Clearing browser cache/data permanently deletes locally stored drawings
Prevention: Use the Share button to save important drawings to your photo library before clearing cache
